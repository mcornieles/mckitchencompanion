Oatmeal Bread – 2 lb

Ingredient Detail Amount Unit Grams
whole milk Room Temperature 1.25 cup 305
unsalted butter Room Temperature, cut into 1/2 inch pieces 2.5 tb 35.5
maple syrup Or Honey 2 tb 40.2
kosher salt 1.25 ts 7.125
flour unbleached Unbleached, All-purpose 3.75 cup 450
oats old fashioned Old fashioned, not quick cooking 1 cup 81
yeast Active dry, Instant or Bread machine 2.25 ts 6.975

1- Put all the ingredients in starting always with the liquids.
2- Press PROG button to select the White program.
3- Press COLOR button to select Crust color.
4- Press SIZE button to select desire loaf size.
5- Press START button to start baking.

NOTE: if you want to delay the process you can press - or + button to delay the process completion.

* For a sweeter bread, increase the maple syrup by 1/2 tablespoon, substitute your favorite granola for the oats, and add 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon.
